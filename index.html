📚 Playing Guide 📚

The goal of the game is to move all cards to the foundations, organized by suit in ascending order (from Ace to King).

How to Play:

  • Click on a card to select it, then click on the spot where you want to move it.
  • You can place cards on top of another card if they are alternating colors and in descending order.
  • The Ace must be placed first in the foundation for each suit.
  • Empty spaces on the tableau can only be filled with a King or a pile starting with a King.

Tips:

  • Try to uncover hidden cards by moving the cards that block them.
  • Prioritize moving cards to the foundations whenever possible.
  • Don't rush; plan your moves to avoid blocking the game.

Have fun and try to win!
